Introducing DetectMe.

Need reasons to love DetectMe? Look no further.

Train your phone to detect objects

It is very simple. Just take your iPhone or iPad and train a new detector using the DetectMe app

Share your detectors

You decide if you want other people viewing and using your detectors. That way your work can help others with their tasks!

Review and extend other detectors

You also can use others public detectors. For example, take a cup detector and extend it to recognize the cups you care about. It's very easy!
